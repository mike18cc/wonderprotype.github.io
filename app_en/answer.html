How does the reward program work?

Q：How does the reward program work?

A：It’s easy – simply browse the offers on our online marketplace, click on the offers that interest you and shop online. After you click thru to the retailer’s online shopping site, you will not see the points you’re earning, but don’t worry, we track the click-thru from our site to the retailer’s site and will provide you points quantity based on how much you spent. Also, if you are  Sprouting an ad blocker software or cookies are not enabled, our offer tracking mechanism won’t work and you will not earn points.
